Macbeth Notes

Definitions

  • Anachronism: a thing belonging to a place where it does not exist
    • Cannons
  • Thane: under king command, varying stratifications of rank, allegiance to king, generals
  • Apparition: a ghost or ghostlike figure

Setting

  • Futile scotland : land ownership
  • 11th century
  • thunder, lightning, doom
  • deserted place, isolate
  • Elizabethan
  • battle taking place between Scotland and Norway- “hurly-burly”:war
  • All actors men

Apparitions

  • A head with armor on and around it
    • Beware Macduff
  • A bloody baby’s head
    • No man born of child can harm Macbeth
  • A child holding a tree branch
    • Macbeth will not die unless Birnam wood moves
  • The future kings which are banquo’s descendants

Character descriptions and events

  • Witches
    • King James ideologies
      • demonology
      • talked about witches walking among people
      • fixation on spiritual aspect
      • Shakespeare works these into his plays
    • Exist on different level than other characters
    • Witches are known to always be evil in the Elizabethan point of view
    • Prophecies for Macbeth
      • Thane of Cawdor
      • King of Scotland
      • Banquo’s son (Fleance) will be king
  • King Duncan
    • 2 sons: Malcolm and Donalbain
    • Killed by Macbeth when congratulating him at his home
    • Brought Lady Macbeth a diamond for her hospitality
  • Malcolm
    • Son of King Duncan
    • After father killed, fleas in fear of being killed to England
    • Alliance with Mcduff and Donalbain to fight against Macbeth
    • Army used camouflage of Birnam wood to move towards Macbeth
    • Macduff brought head to Malcolm and Malcolm promises peace
    • Goes off to city of Scon to be crowned king of scotland there
  • Donalbain
    • Son of King Duncan
    • After father killed, fleas in fear of being killed to Ireland
    • Alliance with McDuff and Malcolm to fight against Macbeth
    • Army used camouflage of Birnam wood to move towards Macbeth
  • Macbeth
    • Scottish general
    • Meets with witches first time that give him eerie prophecies
    • Thane of Glamis - Thane of Cawdor - King of Scotland
    • Kills traitor (Macdonwald) and crowned Thane of Cawdor by king
    • Kills King Duncan (sees bloody dagger prior)
    • Crowned king of Scotland because King dead and sons fled Paranoid
    • Puts hit out for Banquo and Fleance murder, Banquo killed and Fleance escaped
    • Sees ghost of Banquo at a dinner
    • Meets with witches a second time, greeted by reassurance but also reiterate the rise of Banquo’s son
    • Rampage of killing due to paranoia: McDuff’s family killed in this process
    • Birnam wood of Malcolm’s army begins to move toward him, wife committs suicide, verge of defeat, BUT holds on to hope that he can not be killed by a man born from a woman
    • Challenge with Macduff but finds out he was born via C-section and defeated by Macduff
  • Lady Macbeth
    • Wife of Macbeth
    • More ruthless than husband
    • Ready to help fulfill Macbeth’s fate no matter the cost
    • Creates plan to kill King Duncan and frames guards
    • Begins to have doubts/paranoia beginning at the dinner where Macbeth sees ghost of Banquo
    • Committed suicide after tormented by sleepwalking and visions of blood on her hands that she could not get off
  • Banquo
    • Scottish general
    • Macbeth best friend
    • Thane of Lochaber
    • Son: Fleance
    • Killed by hit men of Macbeth
  • Fleance
    • Son of Banquo
    • Able to escape Macbeth’s hitmen
  • MacDuff
    • Finds the dead body of King Duncan
    • Family killed during Macbeth’s paranoia rampage / last straw
    • Alliance with Malcolm and Donalbain to fight against Macbeth
    • Army used camouflage of Birnam wood to move towards Macbeth
    • Challenges Macbeth and reveals he was born via C-section (Not born of woman)
    • Defeats Macbeth
    • Brings head of Macbeth to Malcolm
